Product Overview

People

A next generation HRIS that forms the core of your people stack and programmatically drives your people services, from time off to access management, payroll to benefits. Minimise time spent on admin using automation and employee self-service.

People-centric

Create a better employee experience by building your people operations systems and processes around your people. With self-service and seamless approvals, your team does not have to jump through hoops to get the resources they need.

Enabling

People operations is about providing your team with the right tools and support to get the job done. Neo makes pay, benefits, software, hardware, time off and reviews available in a single place, as simple as drag and drop. So your team can focus on their job rather than learning internal processes.

Automated

Automate the busy work in your day-to-day operations so people can get home on time more often. With Neo you can save time onboarding and offboarding, entering employee information by hand, buying and shipping laptops, creating accounts in work apps, and much more.
